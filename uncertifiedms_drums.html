The UN Certification Assurance

Safety is essential while dealing with hazardous materials. Al Falaah's UN approved drums are UN Certified, which indicates trustworthiness. These barrels are designed to adhere to the strict packing requirements outlined by the UN in the UN Recommendations on the Transport of Dangerous Goods, also known as the UN Orange Book. With this certification, you can be sure that your investments go beyond packaging and include things like the environment, your workforce, and your cargo's safety.

Compliance for each class

There are many categories of dangerous goods, each with its specific set of dangers. The UN Certified MS Drums from Al Falaah are created to meet the requirements of certain classes in particular:

  • - Flammable liquids in Class 3 include substances like petrol, ethanol, and other solvents.
  • - Materials like magnesium and aluminum powder fall into the "Class 4 - Flammable Solids" category.
  • - Class 6 includes substances that can be harmful when ingested, inhaled, or in touch with the skin.
  • - Corrosives are categorized under Class 8 and include acids, alkalis, and other corrosive substances.
  • - Class 9 includes other harmful products that don't fall under other headings.
