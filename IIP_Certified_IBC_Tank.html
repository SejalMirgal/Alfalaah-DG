UN Certified Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBC) Tank:

Our Intermediate Bulk Containers are made of industrial best material, are known for their sturdiness, ease of use and capability to handle pressure & stress during transportation. The Intermediate Bulk Containers are specially designed to handle different kinds of bulk cargo such as flowing liquids, solvents, lubricants, pharmaceutical goods, fertilizers & chemicals, and more.

We provide IBC Tanks with IIP Certificate and UN Certificate. We are leading UN Certified IBC Tanks Supplier

Our safe and sturdy UN Approved Intermediate Bulk Container features are as below:

  • Ensures zero wastage during filling or discharge process
  • Guarantees transfer, free of dust and pollution
  • Serves your storage and transportation needs for a long time without deterioration in quality and quantity.
  • Conforms to international packaging standards.
  • Ensures smooth assembly line operations.
  • Ensures your product is safe
  • Certified as per UN Standards for storage and transportation of Dangerous Goods by Sea.

IIP Certified IBC Tanks are ideal for industries involved in below mentioned sectors:

  • CSafe Working Load (SWL): 400 kilograms. to 1000 kilograms
  • Chemicals and fertilizers
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Solvents and lubricants, and many more
