Compliance as an essential element of shipping radioactive goods:

There are various regulations and compliance requirements for shipping radioactive goods. It must be safe for the environment and human health. Compliance also includes factors like packaging and verification.

Containment is an essential element of shipping radioactive goods.

  • Containment is helpful in reducing the risk related to shipping radioactive goods.

Safe radiation shipment: Navigating challenges in forwarding radioactive goods

Are you looking for a transportation company for radioactive goods forwarding? No more searches are required! It is a challenging task to transport radioactive goods, and any business requires a well-experienced company for shipping radioactive goods. An expert can better help you with safe radiation shipment, but this task includes multiple challenges.

Some challenges are as follows:

  • Packaging: Packaging is one of the most crucial factors in the shipment of radioactive material. A radioactive goods forwarding company assures you of Type A, Type B(U) or Type B (M) packaging. This packaging secures the radioactive goods until delivery.
  • Verifications: Transporting radioactive material worldwide is not an easy task. It requires the verification process from AERB BARC and IATA. We verify if all approvals are in place like AERB NOC, TI, Consignor’s Declaration signed by a Radiologist, etc. and provide safe and secure transportation as per the norms and regulations.
  • Security: Security is a major concern during the transport of radioactive goods. Therefore, proper TYPE Package is used and our dedicated services are committed to the security of people and the environment. Our team members are competent in the safe handling of hazardous goods like radioactive materials.
  • Leakage of hazardous goods: Transporting hazardous goods requires specialized services. There is a risk of radioactive leakage while transporting. Therefore, it is always required to prefer a specialized company that can help you safely forward your hazardous goods.
  • Monitoring:Shipping hazardous goods is not similar to any other delivery. It is the most responsible task, which requires regular monitoring until the goods reach their destination. Our experts monitor the radioactive material throughout the route
